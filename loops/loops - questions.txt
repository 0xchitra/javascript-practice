# 🎯 Concepts Covered

By completing these questions, a learner will practice:

* `for`
* `while`
* `do...while`
* Nested loops
* Infinite loops (and how to avoid them)
* `break`
* `continue`
* Loop counters
* Accumulators (sum, count)
* String traversal
* Number manipulation
* Pattern printing
* Prime numbers
* Fibonacci
* GCD & LCM
* Factors
* Armstrong numbers
* Perfect numbers
* Palindromes
* Factorials
* Real-world loop logic (FizzBuzz & Guessing Game)
